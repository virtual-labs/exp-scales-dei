SCALES

Choose any of the following problems

  1. Construct a scale of 1:50 to read metres and decimetres and long enough to measure 9 m.
  2. Construct a diagonal scale to measure 1/25th and 1/5th of a centimetre. Assume length of scale to be 15 cm.
  3. Construct a vernier scale of R.F. = 1/20 to read metres and decametres and centimetres, long enough to read upto 4 metres. Show a distance of 2.38 metres on the scale.
